TOP 5: HEALTH AND FITNESS

1. Apple Watch Ultra

Price: Approximately $799.00
The Apple Watch Ultra is an advanced sports watch, ideal for extreme adventures and outdoor activities. Equipped with high-precision GPS, blood oxygen monitoring and water resistance, it's perfect for extreme sports. It also has a long battery life and daily health and safety features.

2. Fitbit Charge 5

Price: Approximately $179.95
The Fitbit Charge 5 is a complete activity tracker with enhanced health monitoring, including heart rate, sleep, stress levels and daily activities. It also has built-in GPS and advanced wellness features, such as heart rate variability measurement.

3. Peloton Bike+

Price: Approximately $2,495.00
The Peloton Bike+ is a connected indoor bike that offers an interactive and immersive training experience. With a 23.8-inch rotating touchscreen and enhanced sound, you can take part in live and on-demand classes with professional instructors. Ideal for those looking for a complete workout at home.

4. Theragun Pro

Price: Approximately $599.00
The Theragun Pro is a high-performance electric massager designed for muscle pain relief and recovery after intense exercise. Equipped with a quiet and powerful motor, it has several speed settings and interchangeable attachments to target different muscle areas.

5. Oura Ring

Price: Approximately $299.00
The Oura Ring is a smart ring that monitors sleep health, physical activity and general well-being. It tracks detailed metrics such as body temperature, heart rate and sleep quality, offering valuable insights to improve your lifestyle.
