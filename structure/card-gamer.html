TOP 5: GAMER

1. Razer Naga Pro

Price: Approximately $149.99
The Razer Naga Pro is a modular wireless gaming mouse with three interchangeable side plates, ideal for MMO, MOBA and FPS games. It features Razer optical switches, customizable RGB lighting and up to 20,000 DPI for extreme precision.

2. SteelSeries Arctis Pro Wireless

Price: Approximately $329.99
The SteelSeries Arctis Pro Wireless is a high-quality gaming headset with Hi-Res sound, dual wireless connection for latency-free audio and replaceable battery. It offers long-lasting comfort and crystal-clear audio for total gaming immersion.

3. Logitech G915 TKL

Price: Approximately $229.99
The Logitech G915 TKL is a wireless mechanical gaming keyboard with low-profile GL switches, LIGHTSPEED technology for ultra-fast response and customizable RGB lighting. Its premium aluminum construction makes it durable and stylish.

4. Elgato Stream Deck

Price: Approximately $149.99
The Elgato Stream Deck is an indispensable tool for streamers, with 15 customizable LCD keys that let you control live broadcasts, adjust audio, activate scenes and much more with a single touch. It simplifies and enhances the streaming experience.

5. ASUS ROG Strix XG16AHPE

Price: Approximately $399.99
The ASUS ROG Strix XG16AHPE is a 15.6-inch portable gaming monitor with a 144Hz refresh rate and Full HD resolution.Equipped with NVIDIA G-SYNC technology, it offers smooth, uninterrupted images, perfect for gaming on the go.
