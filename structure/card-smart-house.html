TOP 5: SMART HOUSE

1. Amazon Echo Dot 5

Price: Approximately $49.99
The Amazon Echo Dot 5 is a smart speaker with improved audio, including clearer vocals and powerful bass. Equipped with the Alexa virtual assistant, it allows voice control for music, news and smart home automation. It has integrated sensors, dual-band Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, as well as multiple layers of privacy, such as a button to turn off the microphone.

2. Philips Hue Smart Bulbs

Price: Approximately $81.49
Philips Hue Smart Bulbs are smart bulbs that allow you to control the lighting in your home in a personalized and convenient way. With them, you can choose from 16 million different colors to create the perfect atmosphere in any room.They are compatible with voice assistants such as Alexa, Apple HomeKit and Google Assistant, allowing you to adjust the lighting with just voice commands.

3. Ring Video Doorbell 4

Price: Approximately $199.99
The Ring Video Doorbell is a smart doorbell with 1080p HD video, motion detection, two-way audio and night vision. It allows real-time communication with visitors and sends motion alerts.

4. iRobot Roomba i7+

Price: Approximately $81.49
The iRobot Roomba i7+ is an advanced robot vacuum cleaner that offers intelligent mapping, precise navigation and automatic emptying of the dust collector. It is compatible with voice assistants such as Alexa and Google Assistant, allowing control by voice commands.

5. Nest Learning Thermostat 4ª

Price: Approximately $279.99
The Nest Learning Thermostat 4ª is a smart thermostat that learns your temperature preferences and automatically adjusts them to save energy. It has a larger, clearer screen, motion, temperature, humidity and ambient light sensors, as well as dual-band Wi-Fi connectivity (2.4/5 GHz).
